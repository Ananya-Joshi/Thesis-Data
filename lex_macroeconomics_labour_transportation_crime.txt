in the cases of brushaber v. union pacific railroad (240 u. s. 1) and evans v. gore (253 u. s.. 245). the supreme court pointed out that the sixteenth amendment did  change the law. the sixteenth amendment was  an attempt to change the law. but to provide that thereafter income taxes should  be regarded as direct taxes. and therefore should  be apportioned among the several states in accordance with population. it removed that restriction.
